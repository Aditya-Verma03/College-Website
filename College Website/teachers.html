About Them

Steve Rogers/Captain America

As a former leader of the Avengers and a super-soldier, Steve Rogers could teach courses on combat training, leadership, and ethics. He would bring decades of experience fighting supervillains and leading teams to the classroom.


Hank Pym/Ant-Man

As a scientist and inventor, Hank Pym could teach courses on science and technology. He would be an expert in fields such as robotics, nanotechnology, and artificial intelligence.


Sam Wilson/Falcon

As a former Air Force pararescue and a skilled pilot, Sam Wilson could teach courses on aerial combat and tactical planning. He would be an expert in airborne operations and combat strategy.


T'Challa/Black Panther

As the king of Wakanda and a skilled fighter, T'Challa could teach courses on combat training and tactics. He would bring a unique perspective on fighting styles and strategic planning.


Bruce Banner/Hulk

As a brilliant scientist and the alter ego of the Hulk, Bruce Banner could teach courses on science and technology. He would be an expert in fields such as radiation, biochemistry, and genetics.


Thor

As a powerful Asgardian warrior and member of the Avengers, Thor could teach courses on combat training and tactics. He would bring a unique perspective on hand-to-hand combat and strategic planning.


Clint Barton/Hawkeye

As a former agent of S.H.I.E.L.D. and a skilled marksman, Clint Barton could teach courses on espionage and infiltration. He would be an expert in long-range combat and intelligence gathering.


Doctor Strange

As a master of the mystical arts and protector of reality, Doctor Strange could teach courses on magic and supernatural defense. He would bring a unique perspective on fighting supernatural threats and protecting the multiverse.
